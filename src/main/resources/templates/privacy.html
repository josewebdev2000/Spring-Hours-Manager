Privacy

Security Recommendations

Hours Manager advices customers not to share their credentials with anyone because they could risk losing their account.

Hours Manager advises customers not to share screenshots of their dashboard panels to avoid security violations.

User Controls And Options

Hours Managerlets their users change the username, email, and password of their HoursManager account.

Hours Manager lets their uses export their data in Excel Spreadsheets and PDF Documents.

Cookies and Tracking

Hours Manager provides users the option to use cookies to enhance their experience.

Hours Manager uses strong encryption methods to enhance security.

Data Storage and Management

Hours Manager uses a complex data management model to form relationships between the users' accounts and the data they store.

Hours Manager grants customers the ability to add, read, modify, and delete their data.

User Data Sharing

Hours Manager does not share with any third-party any details of the users' account without their express consent.

Hours Manager is hereby responsible for not sharing customers' data without their consent.
